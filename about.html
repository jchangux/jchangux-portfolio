About me!

Hello! I’m Jessica. Majored in Psychology for undergrad, moved to NYC right after graduation and became a recruiter for 3 years before taking my first step into UX Design! For years I have been so empathetic and compassionate towards other people, always putting myself in their shoes to their thoughts, feelings and actions in every event or situation to the point that I saw it as a personal flaw. I am now unapologetically considerate and have turned my best and worst personality trait into a powerful tool that allows me to adapt and implement the user-centered approach in UX design. My designs stem from the curiosity of user empathy and are data driven. I'm passionate about the multiple ways in which users interact and communicate. I hope to continue to design in the way that continuously puts users first.
